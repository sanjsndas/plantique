Cookies Policy

Effective Date: August 31, 2024

This Cookies Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, the types of cookies we use, and your choices about managing cookies when you access Plantique Nudge Irrigation's website and services.

  1. What Are Cookies?

    Cookies are small text files stored on your device by websites you visit. They help websites recognize your device and remember your preferences or actions. Cookies enhance your browsing experience, providing functionality and personalized content.

  2. Why We Use Cookies

    We use cookies to make our website work properly, improve your experience, analyze usage to enhance performance, and deliver relevant advertising. Cookies also help protect security and detect fraudulent activities.

  3. Types of Cookies We Use - Essential Cookies

    Essential cookies enable core website functions like user authentication and security features. Without these cookies, some services may not work properly.

  4. Types of Cookies We Use - Performance Cookies

    Performance cookies collect information on how you use the website, including pages visited and errors encountered, helping us optimize site functionality and user experience.

  5. Types of Cookies We Use - Functionality Cookies

    Functionality cookies store your preferences like language choice or region, enabling personalized experiences during future visits.

  6. Types of Cookies We Use - Advertising Cookies

    Advertising cookies deliver relevant ads either on our site or across the internet based on your interests. These help us support our services financially while showing more useful promotions.

  7. Third-Party Cookies

    We use third-party services that may set cookies on your device for analytics, advertising, or social media functionality. These are governed by third-party privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

  8. How Long Cookies Remain

    Cookies can be session-based, deleted when you close your browser, or persistent, remaining for a set period. Duration varies by cookie type and purpose.

  9. Managing Cookies

    You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that restricting cookies may affect website functionality or your user experience.

  10. Consent and Cookie Control

    We seek your consent to store non-essential cookies via a cookie consent banner. You can accept or decline these cookies and change your preferences later using site controls.

  11. How We Use Analytics Cookies
